Md. Jewel

Asst. Team Lead, U.S SEC Filings, Wall Street Docs

I am Md. Jewel, currently serving as an Assistant Team Lead for Filings at U.S SEC, stationed at Wall Street Docs (WSD) BD, dedicated to supporting Barclays Bank PLC. Within this role, my primary responsibilities encompass active participation in SEC Edgar filings and crafting tools (leveraging Java and Python) to streamline processes. Additionally, I am deeply involved in the training of new team members. Simultaneously, I engage in cutting-edge ML/DL research at the Center for Applied Research in Software & IT (CARSIT). Previously, I was an integral member of the DIU NLP Research Lab, where I contributed to pioneering research and innovation.

Specializing in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science, I excel in translating concepts into functional code. My passion lies in fusing engineering with AI to create intuitive, scalable applications.
